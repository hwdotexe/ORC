Welcome to MiqoMixers!

A website dedicated to the Final Fantasy: XIV night scene.

What is MiqoMixers?

MiqoMixers is a brand-new, curated review database of Eorzea's greatest roleplay venues. We're on a mission to connect players like you to the best events around. Browse our list of clubs, cafes, concert halls, and more, or suggest a new locale for our team to visit! MiqoMixers is a fan-made database that's always growing and collecting new places to visit.

Our goal is to provide a simple, public list of venues for others to find. We do the work to categorize it so you spend less time scrolling and more time on the dance floor!

How long have you been around?

This website is brand-new, having been live since Summer 2022. If you found this site helpful, we'd love it if you spread the word! Please be patient as we continue fine-tuning our site, as it is still in development.

Are you affiliated with anyone?

MiqoMixers is not affiliated with Square Enix or Final Fantasy XIV in any official or legal way. We're just a bunch of gamers who enjoy the social events put on by other players in the game.

Although we don't partner with or advertise for specific venues, we do accept review requests and compensation. Any reviews that were given such treatment will be denoted with a or tag for transparency.

Can I apply to be a reviewer?

Applications and info on how to become a reviewer can be found on our Discord server at the bottom of the site.

Can you take down a review?

If there is a problem with one of our reviews, please use the "report" link at the bottom of the review. Note that we will only answer takedown requests from the owner of a venue, and we may ask that you prove ownership before we take action.
